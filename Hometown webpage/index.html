Visit Auburn, NY

Auburn is a quaint Upstate NY town with a historical past,
natural lakes, and its own special beauty.

Things to do in Auburn, NY

Take in the history

Visit the Seward House,
Harriet Tubman National
Historical Park, Fort
Hill Cemetery, and more.

Enjoy the nature

Picnic, swim, fish, boat,
jet ski, paddle board, or
kayak, and more on
Owasco Lake.

Broadway in the Finger Lakes

Enjoy quality theater both on
the beautiful shores of
Owasco Lake,
and in downtown
Auburn.
